Vision

Welcome to House of Specials, where every child is celebrated for their unique abilities. We are dedicated to empowering children and adults with disabilities to lead independent and fulfilling lives. Our team of compassionate professionals offers a comprehensive range of therapies, including special education, speech therapy, occupational therapy, social skills training, and sensory integration. At House of Specials, we believe in the potential of every individual. We work with children and adults with autism, ADHD, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, intellectual disabilities, and more. Through our tailored programs, we focus on skill development and holistic growth, ensuring each person we work with can thrive in their everyday lives.
